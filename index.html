Mo'orea's LTER monitoring system

Mo'orea has a long-term environmental monitoring site (LTER) that has been in place for *** years

Biological research at these station has monitored water column nutrient concentrations, among many other datasets, at several sites around the island.

What patterns in nutrient concentration do these monitoring sites illustrate?

Cook's Bay: infrastructure

Cook's Bay is a prime example of built-up areas on the island. Local infrastructure and tourist sites mix together along the bay's shorelines.

Cook's Bay: imagery

Towns and human construction growing along the bay have spead up into the steep slopes leading to the center of the island.

Cleared Vegetation

Patches of cleared vegetations can be found scattered around the island.

Ridgelines

High-slope areas cut across the island only a short distance from the shorelines.

Coral Passages

Passageways have been cut through coral to increase ease of ship passage. Impact on the circulation of nutrients? Currently unknown.
